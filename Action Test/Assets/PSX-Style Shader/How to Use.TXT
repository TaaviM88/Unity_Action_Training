Setup Instructions
1. Attach both scripts 'PSXRetroGlobals.cs' and 'PSXDownscaleDitherEffect.cs' to your game camera(s).
2. Replace your model materials with 'FX/PSX Retro Surface'.
3. That’s it!

Check the demo scene to see the setup in action.

____________________________________________________________________________________________
URP Setup Instructions:

1. Add PSX Downscale Dither Feature to your URP project’s Universal Renderer Data.
____________________________________________________________________________________________
HDRP Instructions:

1. In the project settings-HDRP Global Settings add PSXDownscaleDitherPostProcess in the After Post Process Field.
2. Create a Global Volume/Post-processing/Custom/PSX Downscale Dither Post Process.
3. That's it!
